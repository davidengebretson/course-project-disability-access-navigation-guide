Disability Access Navigation Guide

Performing Arts Center

Building Accessibility Features

  • Elevator
  • Button activated entrances are located on the northeast and southeast sides of the building.
  • The north elevator provides access to floors LL2, LL1, 1 and 2 while the south elevator provides
    access to the dressing rooms and floors 1-3.
  • The academic areas (south end) are accessible via the southeast entrance.
  • Both auditoriums provide wheelchair seating.
  • This building has Accessible, Gender Neutral Restrooms.
